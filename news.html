Structure-Preserving Scientific Computing & ML Summer School & Hackathon

This event brought together 40 graduate students from across the U.S. and Canada, supported in part by the NSF and PIMS. It was an enriching experience to learn, collaborate, and engage with researchers working at the intersection of scientific computing and machine learning.
