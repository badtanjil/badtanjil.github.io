About Me

I am a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering working on machine learning and hurricane simulations. My research focuses on sub-grid scale stress modeling using data-driven methods to improve mesoscale hurricane boundary layer simulations.

Technical Strengths

  • Programming Languages: Python, MATLAB, Fortran
  • Modeling and Analysis: Ansys, Solidworks, COMSOL, AutoCAD
  • Software & Tools: PyTorch, MS Office, LabView
